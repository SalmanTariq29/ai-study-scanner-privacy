01

Overview

AI Study Scanner ("we", "us", or "our") is a mobile application that helps students scan handwritten notes, generate summaries, create quizzes, and build flashcards using artificial intelligence.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We are committed to being transparent — we collect only what is necessary to provide and improve the service.

Short version: We collect your account info, the notes you scan, and basic usage data. We never sell your personal data to anyone. Guest users can use the app without creating an account, and their data stays on-device only.

05

Data Storage & Security

On-device storage: Notes, preferences, and cached data are stored locally using Hive, a lightweight database. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us.

Cloud storage: If you sign in with Google, your notes and progress are synced to Cloud Firestore. Data is stored in Google's secure infrastructure and protected by Firebase Security Rules that ensure only you can access your own data.

Data retention: Your cloud data is retained as long as your account is active. If you sign out and delete the app, on-device data is removed automatically. To request deletion of your cloud data, contact us at the email below.

We do not store your note images. Camera images are processed on-device and immediately discarded after OCR text extraction is complete.
